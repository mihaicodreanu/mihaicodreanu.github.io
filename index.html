About Me

I am a third-year PhD Candidate in the Stanford Department of Economics. My primary research interests are in innovation, entrepreneurship, and firm dynamics. I've completed courses in Labor and Public Economics, IO, and Corporate Finance.

When I'm not debugging code, you can find me trying to avoid bad losses at Stanford Badminton practice. I also love watching (& playing) tennis, and trying to check off as many US states and National Parks as possible while I can still hike and stay up late at night.
